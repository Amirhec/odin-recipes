Crab Boil

Description

This southern dish is a family favorite. It is full of flavor and never disappoints.Crab boil is a spice mixture that is used to flavor the water in which crabs or other shellfish are boiled. Vegetables such as corn can also be used to soak up the flavor leaving each and every last bite savory. Starting in Louisiana the "boil" is now making international headlines. Give it a try with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 red potatoes
  • 8 andouille sausages, sliced
  • 4 onions, quartered
  • 2 ears fresh corn, cut into halves
  • 1/2 cup seafood seasoning(such as Old Bay), or to taste
  • 4 whole Dungeness crabs

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. Add potatoes,sausages,onions,corn, and seafood seasoning. Boil until potatoes are cooked through, about 30 minutes
  3. Gently drop crabs into pot and boil until crabmeat is cooked through, at least 10 minutes. Drain and serve
